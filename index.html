a / about

Between the plan and the decision.

My work sits where statutory policy meets the places people actually live in. I spend my days on development plans, the evidence behind them, and the long process of turning a strategy into something a community can recognise on the ground.

Alongside that, I care about how planning supports local economic development — how regeneration is funded, sequenced, and made to last in the towns that need it most. Good planning is patient, transparent, and accountable, and I try to bring that same posture to the way places are renewed.
